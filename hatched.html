Hatched

Role: Story Board Artists, Animator

Project For: Iat 343 Animation.

Team members: Three other team members

Hatched was a project created for the class iat 343 Animation. I came up with the story board for the film based by a story created by one of our team members. This story board included the story ark of the bird falling out of the nest and getting rescued, as well as the many methods used by the bird to try to make it up into the nest. I also animated several scenes where the bird hatches out of the egg and falls out of the nest. Not all of the scenes I worked on were used in the final film.
