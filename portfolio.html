Design

Sonnetto Application

Completed for the class SIAT 334 Interface Design with collaborators Denis Panda and Joshua Job. Sonnetto was designed as a music jamming application for elderly musicians who can no longer play with each other in person. I completed UX, and interface sketching work on this project.

All Trails Redesign

An assignment for the class SIAT 432. I designed 3 potential new interface interventions for the map function on All Trails. This design was done base on in depth UX work including interviews, and speak allowed interface walkthroughs with test users.

Art

Heaven's Flight

A project completed for a board game jam competition with the project lead Denver. I was responsible for designing card backs and card art for the project, making several draft cards before a we made a major pivot in the project and changed the theme and mechanics of the game.

Game Design

Dolphin Rush

A game submitted to the 2023 Mountain Top Game Jam hosted by the SFU Game Dev club along with Two other team mates. I was responsible for doing art for the game and completed a scrolling background and CG models of mountains. Fish and dolphin assets came from an asset pack.

Raccoon Food Run

A game completed for the GMTK 2024 Game Jam with a team of 5 other people. I contributed a scrolling background for this project and composing the game’s soundtrack. Team member Erica was responsible for the rest of the art and Alex was responsible for animating this art.

Animation

Emily Carr Exercises

A series of 2D and 3D animations completed during my time as a student at the Emily Carr University Animation program. More animations are available on in non html compatible video formats on Vimeo (Link included on the page).

Hatched

An animated film completed for the class SIAT 342 Animation with a team of 4 people. I was responsible for making the story board for this project, and for making two animated scenes where the bird falls out of the nest.

Media

Critical Edge Radio Show

A radio show produced for Emily Carr’s Radio Free Emily by myself and co producer Fernando Ortega .The show included interviews with guests. I was responsible for editing audio on one program and performing at least one interview with a guest. The show ran for two episodes before I transferred to Simon Fraser University.
